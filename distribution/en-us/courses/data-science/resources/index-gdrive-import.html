Teacher Resources

  • Teacher Workbook [PDF] - A downloadable and printable copy of the workbook with solutions for all exercises.

  • PD Workbook [PDF] - A downloadable and printable copy of the student workbook including lesson plans.

  • Online Community [link] - Want to ask a question or pose a lesson idea for other Bootstrap teachers? Want to be kept up-to-date about Bootstrap events, workshops, and curricular changes? Our new discussion forum is the place to do it.

  • What’s Going on in this Graph? [nytimes.com] - Once a month, the NYTimes posts an interesting data visualization. This is a great resource to spark the curiosity of young data scientists, and make for a perfect Notice & Wonder activity!

  • The Pudding [link] - Terrific deep dives into data analysis for interesting topics. Another wonderful resource for students, with great animations showing the transformation of how data is visualized as the research questions evolve.
