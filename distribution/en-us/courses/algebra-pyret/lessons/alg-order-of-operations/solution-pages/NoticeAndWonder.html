Notice and Wonder

Referenced from lesson Order of Operations

Try typing numbers into the Interactions Area, hitting "Enter", and see what you get back! Some ideas:

  1. What is the largest number you can enter? The smallest?

  2. Can you write decimals? Fractions?

  3. After you get back a decimal, try clicking on it. What happens?

  4. Can you write negative numbers? Negative fractions?

  5. What else can you try?

What do you Notice? What do you Wonder?

(sample response) I notice that…​

-fractions are turned into decimals

-if you click on a decimal, you get the fraction back

-decimals are written with a zero before the decimal point

-repeating decimals are barred rather than rounded

-I got tired of making my numbers bigger before the computer did

-negative numbers work
